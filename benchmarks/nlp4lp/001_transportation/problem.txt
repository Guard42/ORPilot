A company ships goods from two warehouses to three customers. Warehouse W1 has a supply of 50 units and warehouse W2 has a supply of 60 units. Customer C1 requires 30 units, customer C2 requires 40 units, and customer C3 requires 30 units. The per-unit shipping costs are: from W1 to C1 costs 2, from W1 to C2 costs 3, from W1 to C3 costs 1, from W2 to C1 costs 5, from W2 to C2 costs 4, and from W2 to C3 costs 8. Determine the shipment quantities from each warehouse to each customer that minimise total shipping cost, subject to supply capacity and demand fulfilment constraints.
