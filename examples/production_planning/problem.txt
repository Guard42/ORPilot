I need to plan production over four months to meet customer demand at minimum cost.

Production parameters:
- Variable production cost: $5 per unit
- Setup cost: $100 per period (charged whenever we produce anything that period)
- Holding cost: $2 per unit held at end of each period
- Maximum production capacity: 50 units per period

Monthly demand:
- Month 1: 10 units
- Month 2: 20 units
- Month 3: 30 units
- Month 4: 20 units

There is no inventory at the start of Month 1 and no inventory should remain after Month 4.

How much should I produce in each month to minimize total cost (production + setup + holding)?
