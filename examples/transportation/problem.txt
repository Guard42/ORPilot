I need to ship goods from two warehouses to three customers at minimum cost.

Warehouse supply:
- W1 can supply up to 50 units
- W2 can supply up to 60 units

Customer demand:
- C1 requires exactly 30 units
- C2 requires exactly 40 units
- C3 requires exactly 30 units

Location coordinates (x, y):
- W1: (1, 4)
- W2: (1, 8)
- C1: (5, 1)
- C2: (8, 6)
- C3: (4, 10)

Shipping cost is $1 per unit shipped per unit of Euclidean distance between source and destination.

How many units should I ship from each warehouse to each customer to minimize total cost?
